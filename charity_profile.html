Volunteer Florida

Tallahassee, FL

Volunteer Florida is the state agency responsible for promoting volunteerism and community service across the Sunshine State. Headquartered in Tallahassee, it connects individuals, nonprofits, and organizations to service opportunities that address Florida’s most pressing challenges. From hurricane relief efforts to mentoring programs in underserved communities, Volunteer Florida empowers citizens of all ages to get involved and make a lasting impact. Whether you’re a student, retiree, or professional, there are countless ways to give back through this dynamic and impactful network.
